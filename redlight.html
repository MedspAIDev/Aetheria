What Is Red Light Therapy?

Red light therapy, also known as photobiomodulation, delivers light energy deep into the skin without heat or damage. It’s commonly used to address a wide range of concerns including:

  • Fine lines and wrinkles
  • Acne and blemishes
  • Joint and muscle pain
  • Scarring and skin damage
  • Inflammation and circulation issues

This treatment is safe for all skin types and requires no downtime, making it an ideal addition to your self-care or wellness routine.
